How it works

Four steps to your first Koble dollar

No sales experience needed. If you can text a friend a recommendation, you can use Koble.

Step 01
Sign up as a Connector
Create your free Koble account. You get 100 unique codes — 80 digital, 20 physical — registered to you and only you.
Step 02
Pick a business you love
Browse Koble-partnered businesses in your city. Choose the ones you genuinely use and recommend to friends already.
Step 03
Share your code
Send a digital code or hand someone a physical card. When they use it in-store, they get a discount and you earn $1 — automatically.
Step 04
Withdraw or invest
Earnings accumulate in your Koble account. Withdraw every quarter, or let them grow with our investment options — up to 2.8% p.a.
